Animals on Endangered Species List

Problem Description

As time goes on, there are more and more species that we have grown up knowing as common animals, that could go extinct. Some of these species on the verge of extinction are essential to the habitat they are a part of and if they do go extinct, some of these habitats will be endangered as well because the species are taken out of the food chain. Even though there are many animals that are on the list, there is a problem that a disproportionate amount of people do not know the animals on the list or how many animals are alive in each species. This crucial information is not reaching large amounts of population, so not much can be done to save the animals. For example, WWF discusses how we are killing these species and the amount of species we are losing. There are other web apps that have attempted to educate people about which species are on the endangered species list. There are a lot of different lists on these other web apps and they give an estimate of the remaining population of each species.

App Description

This application is being created in order to give an opportunity to people who care about animals and want to be able to see what they can do to help these animals. The users of this web app will be anyone who has a special interest in animals, anyone who wants to know about which species are on the endangered species list and anyone who wants to help save endangered species. On this web app, users will be viewing a full list of endangered species and the estimate of the population of each species. This information will mainly come from Animal Welfare Institute.

For this web app, I would like to have a map of the world and for each specie to be catergorized into the region that they are from on the map. With this map, the users would then be able to click on a region of the world (a country or continent) and be able to view a list of all of the different endangered species from that region. This web app will not fully solve the problem of endangered species, but it will help by raising more amounts of awareness towards the issue. The goal is to get more people educated about how species are becoming endangered and what people can do to ensure that more species are not dying out. Also, it will encourage people to do their own research and find ways that they can contribute to the cause and stop species from becoming endangered.
