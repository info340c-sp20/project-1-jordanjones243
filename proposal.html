NBA Analytics

Problem Description

The National Basketball Association is one of the most popular sports in the United States. Many fans love following their favorite teams and all of the news that goes along with those teams. Many people love to look at all of the teams' stats, news and information on their phones or their computers. While there are certain applications that allow you to see each of the stats, news or other team information individually, there is no app that allows you to see all of this in one. For example, NBA Stats shows detailed stats about each NBA team, but it boasts no information about team news, individual player stats or other critical team information.

App Description

This application is being created in order to give an opportunity to fans of the NBA to have one platform that they can get interact with to get all of their team information. The users of this web application will be anyone who is a fan of professional basketball and users that want to keep updated on what is going on in the basketball world. On this web app, viewers will view team stats that will come from the source listed in the previous paragraph, team information such as fan attendance will come from ESPN, and team details will come from websites about each respective team. There will also be news that will be shared on this app that contains information pertaining to the entire NBA, rather than one team.
